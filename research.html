ABOUT ME

My name is Milad Nasri, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science at the University of Kurdistan. I have a strong passion for continuous learning and a commitment to sharing knowledge with others. My primary areas of interest include artificial intelligence, particularly in the fields of machine learning and natural language processing. At present, I am engaged in advanced research focused on text processing and the application of large language models. I have the privilege of working under the guidance of Dr. Parham Moradi from RMIT University in Australia. Through this opportunity, I aim to deepen my understanding of AI technologies and contribute innovative insights to the field. I believe in the transformative power of technology and its potential to enhance human capabilities. Through my academic journey, I strive to not only expand my own knowledge but also to inspire and support others in their learning endeavors.

  • Machine Learning
